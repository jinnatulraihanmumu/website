About Me

Hi! I am Jinnatul Raihan Mumu and I am a final year undergraduate business student majoring in Technology Management with a minor in Information Systems in the Department of Business and Technology Management (BTM) at Islamic University of Technology (IUT) in Bangladesh. As a data and information systems enthusiast, I aspire to be an industry leader in the IT sector.

My previous work experiences extrapolated my skills in crafting solutions for business and gradually gesticulated into an exquisite interest for solving conundrums in the areas of business applications using technology and data as strategies. I have worked on critical business and research projects and shouldered multiple responsibilities at the same time. As I now advance my career in the advanced analytics space, I prioritize learning to acquire and wrangle data using SQL and R programming.

My research interests include Information Studies, Social media and Digital Humanities. Currently, I’ve 12 accepted research papers in Scopus indexed journals like the Journal of Internet Commerce, Applied Nursing Research, International Journal of Industrial and Systems Engineering and in Top conferences like the Hawai’i International Conference on System Sciences (HICSS).

My Technical Skills include:
Programming Languages: SQL, R, C/C++
Business Intelligence Tools: Power BI, Tableau, SSIS
Databases: SQL Server, MySQL, MongoDB
Scientometric/Bibliometric Research Tools: VOSviewer, HistCite, Gephi, Bibliometrix R Package, BibExcel, fsQCA
Machine Learning: Logistic regression, Linear regression, Clustering
Other Tools: Advanced MS Excel, PowerPoint, Weka, Stata

Apart from all this stuff, I would say I seek to be inspired, envision the unlikely, work hard for things that are worth it, and be surrounded by those who bring out the best in me!
